## 5. Technical SEO Issues CrawlBeast Finds Instantly

*   **Broken Paths:** 404 links, 500 server errors, and redirect loops.
*   **Meta & Structure Blindspots:** Missing H1 tags, missing or duplicate meta titles and descriptions, and image alt tag gaps.
*   **Indexing & Linking Hurdles:** Orphan pages (indexable pages with zero internal links) and unmoderated external follow links.
*   **AI Bot Blocker Diagnostics:** Detects configurations (like robots.txt or server-level user-agent blocks) that prevent AI crawlers (ChatGPT and Gemini search bots) from indexing and citing your brand.
*   **AI Engine Schema Gaps:** Incomplete or broken schema markup and viewport rendering issues.
