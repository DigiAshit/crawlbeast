# CrawlBeast - Holding Company Governance (TMaker Model)

## 1. Governance Structure
CrawlBeast is launched and operated entirely under the **TMaker** holding company umbrella.
- **Centralized Finance**: All subscription revenues are processed through TMaker's Stripe account. All operational expenses (concurrent hosting, API credits, proxy services) are paid from TMaker's central funds.
- **No Early Entity Costs**: No separate corporation or banking account is set up for CrawlBeast during the pre-$100k MRR phase.

## 2. Co-Founder Agreement Parameters
- **Partnership**: Split between the Distributor (Founder) and the Builder (Maker co-founder).
- **Equity Split**: 50% / 50% split of net cash profits.
- **No Salaries**: Both partners receive zero baseline salary. Compensation is derived entirely from monthly profit distributions.
- **Monthly Net Profit Distribution Formula**:
  $$\text{Net Profit} = \text{CrawlBeast Subscription Revenue} - \text{Direct Hosting/Proxy/API Expenses}$$
  Distributions are paid directly to co-founders' bank accounts by the 5th of each month.

## 3. The $100k MRR Spin-Off Gate
- In the event that CrawlBeast hits **$100,000 MRR** and sustains it for two consecutive months, either partner can trigger the spin-off.
- TMaker will cover the legal registration costs of the new independent corporation and transfer all CrawlBeast IP, repository code, and customer records cleanly to the new entity.
