# CrawlBeast - Co-Founder Project Agreement (TMaker Model)

This agreement governs the development, operation, and profit-sharing of the software asset **CrawlBeast** under the parent holding company **TMaker**.

## 1. Partnership & Ownership
- **Distributor/Founder**: Ashit Sharma (Ownership: **50%**)
  - *Responsibility*: Distribution strategy, market validation, lead mining, copy, GTM launch, and customer research.
- **Builder/Co-Maker**: Developer Partner (Ownership: **50%**)
  - *Responsibility*: Concurrent crawling engine development, link graph architecture, database maintenance, APIs, and webhook builds.

## 2. Centralized Holding Company Structure
- Both partners agree that CrawlBeast will remain an asset hosted, billed, and operated entirely within **TMaker** during the pre-$100k MRR phase.
- No separate legal entities, LLCs, or independent banking accounts will be formed for CrawlBeast at this stage to avoid legal and administrative overhead.
- All subscription revenues flow to TMaker's Stripe accounts, and all API/hosting costs are paid from TMaker.

## 3. Compensation & Monthly Payouts
- **Zero Salary**: Both partners agree to receive **zero baseline salary**. Compensation is paid strictly as a monthly profit distribution.
- **Monthly Net Profit Distribution Formula**:
  At the end of each calendar month, the net cash profit for CrawlBeast is calculated as:
  $$\text{Net Profit} = \text{CrawlBeast Total Stripe Revenue} - \text{Direct Server/Proxy/API Expenses}$$
  TMaker will distribute the net profit to each co-founder based on their ownership percentage (50% each) within 5 business days of the month-end calculation.

## 4. Intellectual Property & Equity Split Gate
- **IP Assignment**: All code, database tables, alt tags, and digital assets created for CrawlBeast are assigned to TMaker.
- **The $100k MRR Split Clause**: If CrawlBeast reaches **$100,000 Monthly Recurring Revenue (MRR)** and maintains it for two consecutive months, either partner can enforce the creation of a dedicated legal entity. Upon formation, TMaker will transfer all CrawlBeast IP cleanly to the new entity.
